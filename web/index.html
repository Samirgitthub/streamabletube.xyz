StreamtableTube

Stream YouTube videos directly into an HTML5 player.

Example: If you want to stream an audiovisual stream of https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Vhh_GeBPOhs

You would put in the URL: streamabletube.xyz/stream/av/Vhh_GeBPOhs

The av part of the URL can be replaced with a for audio only and v for video only as well.

LIVESTREAMS DO NOT WORK WITH THIS!!!
